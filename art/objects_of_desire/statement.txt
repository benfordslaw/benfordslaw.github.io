<i>program to find out what people want</i><br>
Giant list of things that are wanted, according to authors in project gutenberg born after 1900 <br>
1. Get a single string of all the text from Project Gutenberg written by authors born after 1900 <br>
2. Delete the sentences that don't have anything to do with love or desire or longing <br>
3. Get the direct object out of every clause in which the verb has something to do with love or desire or longing <br>
Put those direct objects into a text file<br>
This resulted in 18,336 things that are wanted.
